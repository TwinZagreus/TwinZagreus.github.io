# Slice transitions after loading

The loading layer should not feel like a blocker. It should feel like a short editorial cut before the interface arrives.

The key is to keep the background alive, then move the foreground away with enough confidence that the route change feels intentional.

## Timing

- Keep the actual exit compact.
- Let the first visual state appear immediately.
- Let the article title arrive from the same direction as the transition line.

## Notes

The slice is not decoration. It is a continuity device between the loading overlay, the homepage, and the writing surface.
