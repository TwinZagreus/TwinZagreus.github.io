# Route transitions without losing calm

A route transition should hide loading and preserve spatial memory.

For this site, the persistent contour field does most of the work. The foreground only needs a line, a soft cover, and a precise title reveal.

## Principle

Do not animate the background away. Keep it alive and move the document surface.
